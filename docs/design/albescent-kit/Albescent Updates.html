The Record

Everything the faction has attended, kept in order. Acknowledged, attended, returned, witnessed — entered quietly, without ceremony, whether or not anyone is reading.

No trace need remain.
The Record exists for those who keep it, not for those who watch. Entries are never ranked, never compared, never scored against another.
